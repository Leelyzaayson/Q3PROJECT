A fire can start by accident, by acts of nature, or even by arson. You can help keep your family and co-workers safe during a fire by making sure smoke alarms work and by practicing a fire escape plan. The resources below can help you learn what to do to prevent fire in your home.

How to prevent fire in your house🔥
Don't play with the matches, especially inside the house.
Secure lighted candles and kept lit candles away from flammable materials such as cloth and wood.
Turn off the stove and remove the pans from burner after cooking.
Regularly check electricals wires for damage. Replace faulty wirings immediately.
Do not overuse outlets.
